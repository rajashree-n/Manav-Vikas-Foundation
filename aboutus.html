Pople Who Care Humanity Manav Vikas Foundation Reg.N0.: E-6516(Thane) A-604, yash Paradise Ctrs., Sector-8A, Airoli, Navi Mumbai 400708, Maharashtra, India
Manav Vikas Foundation is a not-for-profit organisation in Airoli, Navi.Mumbai whose motto is to provide cycles to tribal students, The organization established in 2009 and strives to solve the issues of urban and rural areas as well. The organization is working as a path finder to solve the problems of urban area and contributing to develop rural area. The organization had identified the problems of tribal students of JavhaoWada in Thane District who walk a long distance to reach their schools and took a small step to lift up their moral by Cycle Distribution program.
Mission and Vision :
  • • Photo Through our cycle distribution program our attempt is provide ten thousand cycles to tribal students to enhance their zeal to education for their better future.
  • • It is our endeavor to lift up every tribal student at the grass root level of the society.
Our Reach:
